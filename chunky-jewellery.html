    As we emerge from winter, we're all about levity. Shedding thick knits in favour of airy sheers and floaty meshes is our sartorial focus right now, and offsetting these fabrics with chunky cuffs, weighty chains and sculptural earrings can make for a chic juxtaposition.

    SS24 Jewellery Trends 

    Recent runways dictate that chunky styles still deserve a place on your accessory radar for SS24. Gilded Y-shaped chains stood out at Tom Ford, some skinny, resembling bolo ties, others with a weightier, more artisanal feel, snaking from the collarbone right the way down to the navel, flanked by unbuttoned silky shirts. 

    Also at Tom Ford, ornate, textured cuffs made an appearance, whereas the style was reinterpreted in cool chrome, almost dystopian in sentiment, at Tory Burch.

    Everything was coming up roses at Palomo Spain via the label's supersize creations. Metallic chokers, ear climbers and other iterations brought the classically romantic floral to the fore.

    Similarly at Chanel, statement blooms were on the cards, affixed to chain chokers and styled asymmetrically for offbeat, holiday glamour.

    How we chose:

    • Style and chunkiness: We've scoured the internet's top fashion sites for the chicest pieces of chunky jewellery. They have a thick, weighty appearance, often statement and standout. All our picks either feel bang on trend for SS24 or have a certain seasonless, classic appeal.
    • Price: Whether you're planning on splashing out or bagging a bargain, we've got a piece of chunky jewellery in your price range. We've selected this season's best, ranging from affordable-luxe brands to high-end jewellery labels if you're looking to invest.
