Kourtney Kardashians Maternity Style Is Already Super Edgy

Kourtney Kardashian is known for edgy fashion choices befitting a rock star’s wife, so it comes as no surprise that would translate into her maternity style as well.

A few days after the Poosh founder and her husband, Blink-182 drummer Travis Barker, announced that they’re expecting, Kardashian is already giving fans a glimpse into her pregnancy wardrobe.

In a new Instagram Story post—in which Kardashian implored her followers to share “clean body care” pregnancy recommendations—the reality TV star shared a photo of herself posing next to one of Barker’s drum sets. She wears a body-clinging pale yellow minidress with long sleeves and a black underlay. Over the dress, she layers up with an oversize black varsity jacket. To color-coordinate, she finishes off the look with a pair of Givenchy’s Shark Lock boots in black.

Instagram

Kardashian announced her pregnancy last weekend, when she showed up front-row at a Blink-182 concert holding a sign that read “Travis, I’m pregnant!”

The new addition will join Kardashian and Barker’s blended family. She currently shares three children with ex-boyfriend Scott Disick: 12-year-old Mason, nine-year-old Penelope, and seven-year-old Reign. Meanwhile, Barker shares two kids and a stepdaughter with ex-wife Shanna Moakler: 19-year-old Landon, 17-year-old Alabama, and 24-year-old Atiana.

Soon after the pregnancy announcement, Kardashian posted a portrait to Instagram of her baby bump next to Barker. “Overwhelmed with gratitude and joy for God’s blessing and plan,” she wrote in the caption.
