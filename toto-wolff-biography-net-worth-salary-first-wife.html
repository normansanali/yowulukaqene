Toto Wolff Biography; Net Worth, Salary, First Wife

Toto Wolff, originally known as Torger Christian Wolff, is a former racing driver of Austrian descent. Toto Wolff is an investor and Chief Executive Officer who holds a 33% stake in the Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team.

Toto Wolff commenced his career in motorsport by joining the Austrian Formula Ford Championship and the German Formula Ford Series.

In 1994, Toto Wolff won the “24 Hours Nurburgring” competition. Toto is the founder and owner of technology and computing companies.
