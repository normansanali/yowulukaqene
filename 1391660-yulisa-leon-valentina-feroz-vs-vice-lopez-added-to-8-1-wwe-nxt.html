Yulisa Leon &amp; Valentina Feroz vs. Vice &amp; Lopez Added To 8/1 WWE NXT

Valentina Feroz and Yulisa Leon will be in action on the August 1 episode of WWE NXT.

WWE announced that Yulisa Leon and Valentina Feroz will face Lola Vice and Elektra Lopez on Tuesday. Feroz and Leon were on the winning side of an eight-person tag team match at NXT Great American Bash. There, they teamed up with Dragon Lee and Nathan Frazer to defeat The Meta-Four.

Aside from a battle royal, Tuesday’s match is set to be Vice’s first standard bout on NXT. She has competed multiple times on NXT Level Up.

Updated Card For WWE NXT

The card for the August 1 episode of WWE NXT is as follows:

  • Yulisa Leon and Valentina Feroz vs. Lola Vice & Elektra Lopez
  • Lyra Valkyria vs. Jacy Jayne
  • Eddy Thorpe vs. Dijak
  • Schism Interrogation

WrestleZone will have coverage of NXT as it airs on Tuesday.
