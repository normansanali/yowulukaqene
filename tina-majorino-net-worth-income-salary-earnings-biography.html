Tina Majorino Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Tina Marie Majorino (/medrino/; born February 7, 1985) is an American actress who has appeared in films and on television. When she was a young actor, she appeared in films such as Andre, When a Man Loves a Woman, Corrina, Corrina, and Waterworld. She went on to become a successful actress. Despite her early success, she took a break from 1999 to 2004 to recharge her batteries. She returned for one of her most well-known performances to date, as Deb in the 2004 picture Napoleon Dynamite, which became a critical and commercial success. Majorino has been in a number of television shows since returning to the industry, including Veronica Mars, Big Love, True Blood, and Grey’s Anatomy. Ms. Majorino was cast in the role of Mac in the Veronica Mars film and has also appeared as Maggie Harris in the TNT television series Legends of Tomorrow.
