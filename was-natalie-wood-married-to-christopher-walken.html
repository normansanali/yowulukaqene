Was Natalie Wood married to Christopher Walken?

Natalie Wood was an American actress best known for her performance as a child in Miracle on 34th Street (1947).

Natalie Wood was born Natalie Zacharenko, on July 20, 1938, San Francisco, California, United States to  Maria Zudilova, and her second husband and carpenter Nicholas Zacharenko.

Natalie Wood began her acting career at age four. She was given a co-starring role at age 8 in Miracle on 34th Street (1947). Natalie Wood was the recipient of four Golden Globes and three Academy Award nominations.

Natalie Wood died off the coast of Santa Catalina Island on November 29, 1981. She passed away at age 43 and was on a holiday break from the production of her would-be comeback film Brainstorm (1983).

Was Natalie Wood married to Christopher Walken?

No, Natalie Wood was not married to Christopher Walken. The pair happened to be only co-stars and friends.
