Following his gorgeous girlfriend’s death on Christmas Day 2006, Ryan Waller became the subject of intense national attention. Let’s get to know more about him.

Who Happened To Ryan Waller?

On December 25, 2006, Ryan Waller and Heather Quan were shot and assaulted at their residence. The adorable couple was getting ready for Ryan’s father’s pre-Christmas dinner. Though they weren’t expecting anyone, they heard a knock at the door. When Ryan Waller went to investigate, he saw his father, Larry Lloyd Carver, holding a gun along with Richie Lee Carver, his former flatmate. 

He opened the door, but not quickly enough. After shooting him in the head through his left eye, the assailants thought he was dead and shot his girlfriend, Heather, in the adjacent room. Richie and his father Larry seized a computer and some firearms from the house after beating the couple.

After not hearing from Ryan or Heather for two or three days, Ryan’s father started to worry. The cops reacted to his call right away and made their way to Ryan and Heather’s home. Ryan Waller was still alive, but Heather Quan had been found dead by the police.

Rather than phoning for emergency medical assistance, Ryan was arrested by the police. After spending almost four hours sitting in the police cruiser, he was brought to the interrogation room at five the next morning. Ryan Waller was still in excruciating pain and gravely injured when Detective Paul Dalton questioned him.

Ryan indicated he wanted to go to sleep and said very little during the interrogation. He was able to describe the assailants. He didn’t, however, seem to be willing to admit that he shot and killed Heather, as the investigator had requested. 

His father was told that he was in critical condition by the police, who summoned the ambulance after many hours of questioning. According to the doctors, Ryan developed a potentially fatal infection as a result of not receiving emergency care. Ryan Waller lost a portion of his brain and his left eye during the 35-day recovery period. Ryan had seizures for a number of years after that.
