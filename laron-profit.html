Career

Profit was picked 38th overall in the second round of the 1999 NBA draft by the Orlando Magic. Profit aspired to work as a professional shooting guard. Profit was traded to the Washington Wizards on September 22, 1999, in exchange for a second-round draft pick in the 2001 NBA draft. From 2002 to 2004, Profit will tour worldwide. Profit averaged 13.7 points, 2.8 rebounds, 1.2 assists, and 2.2 steals in six games with Premiata Montegranaro of the Italian Lega Basket Serie A beginning in January 2002. Profit re-signed with the Washington Wizards as a free agent on October 1, 2004. Profit and Kwame Brown were traded to the Los Angeles Lakers for Caron Butler and Chucky Atkins on August 2, 2005. Profit was signed to a one-month contract with a full-season option by Anadolu Efes S.K. of the Turkish Basketball League on December 12, 2006.
