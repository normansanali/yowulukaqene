Rebecca Romijn, Jerry O'Connell, Twins, Pets in Matching Christmas PJs

One big happy family! Rebecca Romijn and husband Jerry O’Connell got super duper festive in their Christmas photoshoot with twin daughters Dolly and Charlie, both almost 5. The adorable brood — three dogs and two cats included — posed for their holiday photoshoot while wearing matching mint green pajamas. 

Romijn, 41, shared pictures of the holiday card and some behind-the-scenes shots on Twitter. The family’s two cats look less-than-pleased to be put in the tight-fitting PJs, but the dogs happily lick the little girls’ faces in one shot. 

“Happy holidays from all nine of us!!” Romijn captioned one shot.

Romijn and the former Nip/Tuck star, 39, have been married since July 2007. They got engaged in September 2005, just six months after she divorced her first husband, actor John Stamos. The couple welcomed their twin daughters in late December 2008.
