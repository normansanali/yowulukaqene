Sibel Kekilli Net Worth

Sibel Kekilli net worth:

$3 million

Sibel Kekilli is a German actress. She started her acting career in the mid 2000s on German screen. Her debut was in the movie Head-On. Her performance in the film earned her many Best Actress awards. She subsequently starred in other German features, Kebab Connection and Winter Journey. Her first international project was in Fay Grim. She went on to star in the German movies The Last Train, Eve Dönüş and Pihalla. Her performance in the German-Turkish movie When We Leave earned her acclaim. In the 2010s, she became known internationally for playing Shae on Game of Thrones.
