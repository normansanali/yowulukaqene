Early Life and Family

Andrea Nicole Guck Eigenmann, better known as Andi Eigenmann, was born in Marikina, Philippines, on June 25, 1990. She is of Filipina descent and of mixed race, since her mother has German-American lineage and her father has Swish-Spanish-German background. Her race is white, and her religious beliefs are Christian.

Andi turned 31 in the year 2021, and her star sign is Cancer, according to her birth date. She is the daughter of actors Mark Gil (father) and Jaclyn Jose (mother), and the half-sister of Mark Gil’s actors Sid Lucero, Gabby Eigenmann, and Max Eigenmann.
