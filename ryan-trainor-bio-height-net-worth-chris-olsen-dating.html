For a while, now TikToker Chris Olsen and Meghan Trainor’s brother Ryan Trainor have been rumored to be dating. So, in the rest of the writing, let us tell you what is actually the truth. Meet Ryan Trainor, Meghan Trainor’s Brother

The world sees Ryan Trainor as the older brother of his pop superstar sister, who packed up and left his hometown to help support her carve her career in Los Angeles. Also, people remember him as someone who gave Meghan the nickname “MTrain” which today the diva’s fans chant while she is on stage.

Also, Ryan is someone who has partnered with his famous sister in the podcast Workin’ On It with Meghan Trainor & Ryan Trainor. Here, the siblings get more honest than ever before. They take on a new topic each week, and share their stories while also inviting listeners into the conversation. With him and friends, Meghan discusses her journey from becoming a new mom, to navigating Hollywood, to mental health, and everything in between. Ryan also gets into the nitty gritty of his life. “Prepare to laugh, cry, and hopefully learn something new”, this is how the program’s executive producers, Meghan and Ryan, can be seen teasing the show on the internet.

Over the years, Ryan together with his other brother has had a first-hand glimpse of their sister’s rise to superstardom. And through it all, they have shown her immense support.

Meghan often shows glimpses into their tight sibling bond. For instance this one time, she showed a reaction of her, i.e, literally crying, when Ryan surprised her with “crazy” Christmas decorations. Meghan, who has always been obsessed with Christmas and lights, called Ryan “the best big brother in the world”.

Are Ryan Trainor And Chris Olsen Dating?

After making people believe for a while about his romancing Ryan, Chris Olsen finally decided to come clean and clear of the gossip.

On March 12th (in 2023) Chris posted photos of himself kissing Ryan, on his IG post. These images included them locking lips passionately atop a bridge. And this naturally caused reactions from fans. Also, these intimate seeming pictures were included in a photo dump from Chris’s time in Australia with Ryan and his family, including Megan.

After almost a month, on April 3rd, Chris finally decided to clear up the fact that they are not dating. In this video he posted online he was seen dancing with Ryan to Meghan’s track “Mother” when he unveiled the highly anticipated truth about their alleged romance. He said he and Ryan are not together and are still very separated and single. Of course, but fans were still not convinced. They rushed to the internet to comment that Chris had missed April Fools Day by a couple of days.

Ryan is the eldest of three kids born to his parents Kelli and Gary Trainor of Nantucket, Massachusetts. Meghan is the middle child and the youngest Justin Trainor was born last on 24 January 1996. John reportedly has been pursuing a career as a music producer.

Back in August 2021 too, Chris kind of hinted at a no-romance with Ryan saying he is willing to play matchmaker for Ryan who is “single”. Not to miss, he also informed people that he is for the girls.

How Old Is Ryan Trainor?

Ryan was born in 1992. So, he turned 30 years old in 2022.

Ryan Trainor Job

Ryan Trainor is a Twitch streamer and an aspiring YouTuber among other things. Then, we can see IMDB crediting him as the music director of several soundtracks and music videos. For instance, the soundtrack “Good To Be Alive” from the movie The Peanuts Movie from back in 2015.

Not to miss, together with his brother Justin, Ryan actually joined Meghan in the studio a few times. Both are named as co-writers and backup vocalists on her new album Treat Myself. The brothers also played a role in their sister’s Christmas album A Very Trainor Christmas. On this one particular, Ryan wrote and Justin in addition to writing also oversaw production. On this very music video their cousins Jayden, Jenna, Marcus Toney, and father Gary, also appear as featured artists.
