Does Kirk Herbstreit have a brother?

Kirk Herbstreit is a 52-year-old American sports analyst who works on the “College GameDay” television program on ESPN. Kirk Herbstreit played college football as a quarterback for Ohio State football before venturing into sports analysis.

Kirk Herbstreit provides analysis on college football games and also provides commentary on college football games. Kirk Herbstreit is a two-time Outstanding Event Analyst winner, five-time Sports Emmy Awards winner and a three-time Outstanding Studio Analyst winner.

Does Kirk Herbstreit have a brother?

Kirk Herbstreit was born to Judy Herbstreit and Jim Herbstreit, and he does not have a brother. Kirk Herbstreit rather has a sister called Terri Holmes.

Kirk Herbstreit graduated high school from Centerville High School and proceeded to the Ohio State University to major in Business Administration. Kirk Herbstreit is married to Alison Butler Herbstreit and he is blessed with four children: Jake Herbstreit, Tye Herbstreit, Chase Herbstreit and Zack Herbstreit.
