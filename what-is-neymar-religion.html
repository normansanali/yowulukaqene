What is Neymar religion?

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior, better known by his stage name Neymar, was born on February 5, 1992. He plays forward for both the Brazil national team and Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain.

Neymar da Silva Santos Junior was born in Mogi das Cruzes, So Paulo, as the son of Neymar Santos Sr. and Nadine da Silva. The nickname he goes by today was given to him by his father, a former football player who later served as his son’s mentor when his skills improved.

He is regarded as one of the best players in the world due to his prodigious goal-scoring and renowned playmaking abilities. Only three players in history have scored at least 100 goals for three separate clubs. Neymar is one of them.

Neymar first rose to fame when he made his league debut for Santos at the age of 17. He contributed to the team’s triumph in the 2011 Copa Libertadores, a Copa does Brasil, and back-to-back Campeonato Paulista championships. First Copa Libertadores title for the squad since 1963. In the 63rd minute of Barcelona’s 7-0 victory over Levante in the opening La Liga game of the 2013–14 season, Neymar made his club debut. He replaced Alexis Sanchez.

Prior to making the most expensive move in history on August 3, 2017, when he joined Paris Saint-Germain for a cost of €222 million (the amount of his contract’s release clause), he spent four years at Barcelona.
