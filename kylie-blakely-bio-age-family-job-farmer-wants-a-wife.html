Kylie Blakely Age, Family, Job

Kylie Blakely from Farmer Wants A Wife announced her exit. She made quite an impression on her partner Landon Heaton for her work.

The article covers information about her parents, age, job, and more via this Wikipedia-styled bio. So, delve into this article to find out more about Kylie.

Kylie Blakely On Farmer Wants A Wife

Kylie Blakely on Fox’s original reality dating series Farmer Wants a Wife, charmed her way into Landon Heaton’s heart from the beginning. Landon praised Kylie’s work as an advocate for veterans’ issues in Orlando, Florida, outside of the show. Furthermore, Kylie acknowledged that she was one of the few ladies on the show with prior cattle drive experience during a spontaneous interview on Landon’s property.

Unfortunately, Kylie and Landon from Farmer Wants a Wife just weren’t meant to be. As previously reported by Distractify, there were no animosities between the two. Landon simply didn’t want to stop Kylie from doing the vital job she does outside of the show, and he thought that if she moved in with him, it would stop her from doing her advocacy work in real life.

In a recent post, Kylie added, “I’ve thought long and hard about how I want to go about this… Being a part of #FarmerWantsAWife was truly a dream come true, I couldn’t have gotten to where I am without Tina, Ahmad & our countless phone calls leading up to the moments that I had no idea would change my life forever. From the moment I got to Nashville and started meeting some of the amazing women you’ve all grown to love on the show I knew I’d walk away with a farmer, some new best friends or both! My time on the farm and traveling with you girls to the mixers are times I’ll cherish for the rest of my life.”

On her Instagram page, Kylie only had positive things to say about her farmer ex, writing in one tearful farewell message (you know, the one ousted contestants make after their exit episode airs), “You’re [Landon] a good man and your mama did a good job raising you and an even better job of teaching you how to dance! God brings people together sometimes for lessons and you were one of mine.”

She added, “I know my forever is somewhere out there and I know God will bless you and whoever you choose!” Kylie thanked Fox for the “adventure of a lifetime,” and said she couldn’t wait for the finale.

Kylie Blakely Family

Kylie Blakely is the daughter of Michelle Watson Blakely and Stan Blakely. Her parents have been happily married for 27 years. In fact, they exchanged their wedding vows in 1995 on Stan’s birthday which is on 9 September (1966).

On their 27th anniversary, Michelle posted on her Facebook, “Missed posting yesterday but Stan had a Happy Birthday and Happy 27th Anniversary Babe!! YES, we got married on his Birthday!! Thank you for all the posts, texts, and calls. We are so blessed to have all of you in our lives.”

Stan is currently 56 years old. Michelle on the other hand is currently 51 years old.

Michelle is employed at Friends Salon and Spa and Idoweddingsandmore.com. She is an Esthetician and Make-up artist by trade. You can find her here (@michellemaree.esthetician). Stan is a district manager at Ecolab since 2002.

How Tall Is Kylie Blakely?

Kylie Blakely Job

Kylie Blakely is working as the Chief Executive Officer of Why Bae LLC since 2020. Her business focuses on mental & physical well-being. She has also been a Student at the University of Central Florida since January 2020.

Kylie’s career began as a Golf instructor at Harmony Golf Club & Community in October 2015. She then dabbled into fitness by working as a fitness consultant at Orange Theory Fitness. She served as Group Fitness Instructor at Crunch Fitness.

From 2019 to 2022, Kylie worked at EcoSun Homes. Initially, the company hired her as a sales marketing administrator. Starting from February 2020 she started working as full-time Marketing And Public Relations Specialist.

In 2022, she also dabbled into pageantry. She became Miss Pinellas County 2022-23. She was also candidate no. 5 for Miss Florida.

About her education, at the University of Central Florida, Kylie is a Bachelor of Applied Science – BASc, Pre-Medicine/Pre-Medical Studies student. She holds AA degree from Valencia College.

Furthermore, she is a licensed CPR, Group Fitness Instructor, and Certified Personal Fitness Trainer-Exercise and Fitness Specialist (CPT-EFS). Whereas, she has volunteered as Community Volunteer (Homes for Heroes®) and student volunteer (Camaraderie Foundation and USO).
