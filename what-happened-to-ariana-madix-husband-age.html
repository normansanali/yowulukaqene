Ariana Madix is an American TV personality, Actress, and author best known for her role on the reality TV show Vanderpump Rules. She was born on 1985 June 24. She is 37 years as of 2023.

The actress was born and raised by her parents, Jim and Tanya, in Melbourne, Florida.

Madix graduated with a theatre and broadcast journalism degree from Flagler College in St. Augustine, Florida.

In 2013, Madix moved to Los Angeles and was cast in the second season of Vanderpump Rules, a reality show that follows employees’ lives at SUR, a restaurant owned by Lisa Vanderpump.

She has also co-authored a cocktail book with her Vanderpump Rules co-stars, Fancy AF Cocktails.

What Happened To Ariana Madix?

Ariana, a reality star on Vanderpump Rules, has shared her experience with Cancer in hopes that her fans will take their health seriously.

In late 2018, Madix was diagnosed with stage 1 melanoma and had to undergo surgery to remove a tumour.

After the surgery, she proudly showed off her scar at the Heart Radio Alter Ego 2019 music festival. And later, she became an ambassador for The Skin Cancer Foundation, using her platform to raise awareness about skin cancer’s dangers.

Madix shared her Cancer scare with her fans on Instagram, revealing that she had a cancerous mole removed from her body and lymph nodes biopsied.

Madix’s proactive approach to her health inspires her fans, especially those hesitant to seek medical care. She encourages those facing a similar situation to look into The Skin Cancer Foundation for information and resources.

In February 2022, Madix also opened up about her struggles with an eating disorder when she first joined Vanderpump Rules. She shared that she has since recovered and hopes to inspire others struggling with similar issues to seek help and prioritize their health.

Ariana Madix Husband 

Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval, both cast members of the popular reality show Vanderpump Rules, were in a romantic relationship for almost nine years.

They made their relationship official on February 10, 2014, by sharing the news on Instagram.

Throughout their relationship, the couple has shared several milestones. They purchased their first home together in Valley Village, California.

They also co-author their first book, Fancy AF Cocktails: Drink Recipes from a Couple of Professional Drinkers, released on December 3, 2019. Even though they have been together long, Madix has clarified that she doesn’t want to get married or have kids.

Unfortunately, After almost nine years together, the couple broke up because Madix discovered that Sandoval had cheated on her with their co-star Raquel Leviss.

Ariana Madix Net Worth

As of 2023, Ariana Madix’s estimated net worth is $1 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. She has earned her income through various sources over the years.

Madix began her career by appearing in videos for the comedy website CollegeHumor. She also landed guest roles on several television series, including Anger Management, Dads, Single Siblings, and Waking Up with Strangers.

She has also acted in films such as Killer Eye: Halloween Haunt, Working It Out, Dirty Dealing 3D, Attack of the 50-Foot Cheerleader, and Dead End. All of these acting roles likely contributed to her net worth.

Madix gained further fame by working at Lisa Vanderpump’s restaurants, SUR and Villa Blanca, which led to her being cast in the reality television series Vanderpump Rules in 2013.

She has since become a leading cast member on the show, and her popularity on the show likely resulted in additional income through endorsements, sponsorships, and appearances.

In November 2017, Madix released a holiday lip kit, “Ariana Nudist Lip Set”, with Frankie Rose Cosmetics.

In 2019, Madix and her partner Tom Sandoval released their cocktail book Fancy AF Cocktails: Drink Recipes from a Couple of Professional Drinkers.

The book was published by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt and may have been another source of income for her.
