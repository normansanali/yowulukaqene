About

Famous as the lead singer of the California-based rock and pop band Allstar Weekend, Porter recorded two albums with the group, Suddenly Yours and All the Way, before he and his bandmates parted ways.

Before Fame

In 2007, he became part of a band called Outerspace Politicians; the group later changed its name to Allstar Weekend and ascended to fame following a performance on Radio Disney's Next Big Thing talent competition.

Trivia

He joined Twitter in 2009 and has earned over 50,000 followers. His band's even more popular YouTube channel, which was launched in 2008, accumulated over 80,000 subscribers and close to 24 million individual views.

Family Life

He was born and raised in the San Diego suburb of Poway, California.

Associated With
