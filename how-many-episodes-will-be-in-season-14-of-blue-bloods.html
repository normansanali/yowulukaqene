How many episodes will be in season 14 of Blue Bloods?

Blue Bloods, the beloved police procedural television drama, continues to captivate audiences with its multi-generational family of law enforcement officers committed to serving New York City.

As fans eagerly anticipate the remaining episodes of Season 14 and its return in Fall 2024, let's take a deep dive into the details of the upcoming episodes and the total number of episodes in this season.

Season 14 Episode Guide

Season 14 of Blue Bloods consists of a total of 10 episodes. The episodes are as follows:

  • Loyalty (S14 E1) - Directed by Alex Zakrzewski, this episode aired on February 16, 2024, and garnered 5.67 million U.S. viewers.
  • Dropping Bombs (S14 E2) - Directed by Jackeline Tejada, this episode aired on February 23, 2024, and attracted 5.45 million U.S. viewers.
  • Fear No Evil (S14 E3) - Directed by Ralph Hemecker, this episode aired on March 1, 2024, and received 5.55 million U.S. viewers.
  • Past is Present (S14 E4) - Directed by Bridget Moynahan, this episode aired on March 15, 2024, and drew 5.47 million U.S. viewers.
  • Bad Faith (S14 E5) - Directed by Robert Harmon, this episode aired on April 5, 2024, and obtained 4.86 million U.S. viewers.
  • Shadowland (S14 E6) - Directed by Jackeline Tejada, this episode aired on April 12, 2024, and captured 5.41 million U.S. viewers.
  • On the Ropes (S14 E7) - Directed by Doug Aarniokoski, this episode aired on April 26, 2024, and garnered 5.38 million U.S. viewers.
  • Wicked Games (S14 E8) - Directed by Ralph Hemecker, this episode aired on May 3, 2024, and attracted 5.16 million U.S. viewers.
  • Two of a Kind (S14 E9) - Directed by Doug Aarniokoski, this episode is set to air on May 10, 2024, with the viewership yet to be determined.
  • The Heart of a Saturday Night (S14 E10) - This upcoming episode is scheduled to air on May 17, 2024, marking the season finale of Part 1 of Season 14.
