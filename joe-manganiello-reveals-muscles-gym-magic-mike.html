Joe Manganiello flexes his muscles ahead of Magic Mike sequel

    Sofia Vergara is a lucky lady. Her boyfriend Joe Manganiello shared a photo from a recent trip to the gym – and it's clear all his hard work is paying off. The 37-year-old actor is notoriously fit, and his incredibly impressive arm muscles are on display as he works out with a dumbbell.


    Joe has a reason for keeping in especially good shape – in two weeks he will  start filming his role for . The highly-anticipated sequel about a troupe of male strippers will see hanning Tatum reprise his role as  Michael 'Magic Mike' Lane. It is also thought that original cast member Alex Pettyfer is back on board for the film, which is scheduled for release in July 2015.
