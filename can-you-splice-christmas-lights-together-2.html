Can you splice Christmas lights together?

Never splice multiple strands of Christmas tree lights together unless you are sure the wire is rated to carry the voltage safely. If the wires for your Christmas tree lights have a damaged portion, you may want to cut the wire to remove that portion and splice the good pieces of wire back together.Click to see full answer. To make the 60 bulb string more aesthetically pleasing, I would like to cut off the end plug/sockets and solder the strands together into one continuous line. This would leave just one 3 amp fuse at the start of the string. can you cut LED string lights? NEW style of tiny LED string lights which are waterproof so they can be used under water and are amazing! Lights can be cut down to any desired length just by using a pair of scissors. Keeping this in view, can you cut LED Christmas lights to length? The lights are designed so each bulb receives all 120 volts and each bulb has a direct route to power, meaning they can be cut to any length without overpowering and burning out bulbs. With the lights unplugged cut wires with wire cutters between the bulbs and cap each end with wire connectors.What gauge wire is Christmas lights? The lower the gauge, the thicker the wire. Mini lights use 22 gauge wire. C7s and C9s generally use 18 gauge wire. Specialty bulbs have a large base that requires them to be on 12 gauge wire.
