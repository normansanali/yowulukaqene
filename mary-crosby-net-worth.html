Mary Frances Crosby Net Worth

Mary Frances Crosby is an American actress with a net worth of $5 million. Crosby was born in Los Angeles, California, and is the daughter of Bing Crosby and his second wife, actress Kathryn Grant. She graduated from high school at 15 and later dropped out of the University of Texas at Austin to pursue an acting career.

Crosby began her professional television career in 1969 with a role on “The Danny Thomas Hour” and made her film debut in “Goldilocks and the Three Bears” in 1970. She continued to work steadily in both film and television throughout the 80s and 90s, but is best known for her role as Kristen Shepard on “Dallas.”

The episode in which it was revealed that her character shot J.R. Ewing remains one of the most-watched episodes in television history.
