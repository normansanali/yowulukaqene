Carrie Underwood Shares a Photo Son at Jacksonville Zoo

Carrie Underwood is one busy mom! The country superstar is currently performing around the country for her Storyteller Tour, and her jam-packed schedule doesn't always leave much time for her to spend with her 10-month-old son Isaiah while she's working. Thankfully, she has some pretty adorable pictures that show what he's been up to all day.

Underwood took to Instagram to share a recent photo of her son at the zoo in Jacksonville, Flor., and as you might expect the snap is very cute. "While Mommy was at work today, the little man made a new friend!" she captioned the shot, which shows the tot sitting on the railing looking at the tigers in a striped hoodie, jeans, and sneakers (below).
