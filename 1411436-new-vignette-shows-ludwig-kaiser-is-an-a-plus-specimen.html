New Vignette Shows Ludwig Kaiser Is An A-Plus Specimen

A new vignette from tonight’s episode of WWE RAW showed Ludwig Kaiser in a new light, as a specimen comparable to various artworks.

The latest vignette from WWE showcased Ludwig describing himself as an “A-Plus specimen,” complete with various footage of him in the ring.

Besides the in-ring footage, Kasier’s voiceover was also accompanied by images of various famous artworks, and then what looked to be A.I. renderings of Kaiser as the artwork itself.
