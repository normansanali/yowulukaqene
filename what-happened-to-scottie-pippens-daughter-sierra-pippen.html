What Happened To Scottie Pippens Daughter Sierra Pippen?

Scotty Maurice Pippen Sr., also known as Scottie Pippen, was an American former professional basketball player who was born on September 25, 1965.

He was a 17-year veteran of the National Basketball Association (NBA), earning six titles with the Chicago Bulls. During the 1990s, Pippen and Michael Jordan were instrumental in developing the Bulls into a championship team and popularizing the NBA around the world.

Scottie Pippen, the former Chicago Bulls player, has made plenty of headlines for his basketball abilities, but at home, he’s just Dad.

Several of the gifted athlete’s prior partnerships have produced eight children, including four from his marriage to Larsa Pippen, and several of them have followed in his athletic footsteps.

What Happened To Scottie Pippen’s Daughter Sierra Pippen?

Sierra went to college at the University of Iowa and was charged with public urination and intoxication after being arrested at the Sheraton Hotel near the Iowa campus.

She reportedly was arrested at the same place two weeks earlier for getting into a fight with hotel staff. According to her Instagram, she’s an artist consultant for UnitedMasters.

In a tribute after Kobe Bryant passed away, she said, “I didn’t have the luxury of enjoying The Bulls during the ’90s so Kobe Bryant was my Michael Jordan.” She also regularly posts pictures of her mother, with whom she seems to be very close.
