Charles Osgood Wife: Who Is Jean Osgood? CBS Stalwart Charles Osgood Wife

Jean Osgood, CBS Stalwart Charles Osgood Wife

The longtime CBS News host, also fondly called the CBS Newsman, Charles Osgood has passed away at the age of 91. The bow-tie-wearing former anchor suffered from Dementia and spent his last days at his home in New Jersey. Osgood quite remarkably pulled off the “CBS Sunday Morning” for almost two decades.

Apart from being a successful news professional, Osgood lived a happy married life. He was married to Jean Osgood for over 50 years. And, the couple tied the knot in 1973. The couple gave birth to five children together and the eldest one among them, Kathleen Osgood, a year into their marriage.

Who Is Jean Osgood?

Jean Osgood is the second wife of Charles Osgood, a famous news personality and radio show host. Charles married Jean in 1973 after he got separated from his first wife. Not much is known about Jean publically as the couple made very few appearances together.

Jean has also not spoken much about either herself or the relationship he shared with Charles. However, in 1999, Charles answered the Proust Questionnaire for Vanity Fair and declared Jean to be his greatest love.

She loved his company in their south of France home and was always at his side in their New York home, according to CBS. Jean further disclosed that when he asked her out to a basketball game, she turned down his invitation for their first date.

Charles has left behind a great legacy. Not only did he succeed in his career as a host but also as a radio presenter.
