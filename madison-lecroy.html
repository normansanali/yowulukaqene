Madison LeCroy News - Us Weekly

Name:Madison LeCroy

Hometown:Greenville, South Carolina

Birthday:October 6, 1990

About

Madison LeCroy was born in Greenville, South Carolina, on October 6, 1990. She attended the Carolina College of Hair Design and went on to become a hair and makeup artist in Charleston, South Carolina. She owns a salon in the area.

LeCroy made her debut on Southern Charm during season 6, which aired in 2019, after she began a relationship with cast member Austen Kroll. The pair dated on and off until December 2020, when she exclusively revealed to Us Weekly that they had split for good.

LeCroy made headlines in January 2021 after she sparked romance rumors with Jay Cutler amid his divorce from Kristin Cavallari. The courtship was short-lived, as later that month LeCroy made her feud with Cutler and Cavallari public knowledge by releasing alleged text messages from the retired athlete. LeCroy again turned heads in February 2021 after she detailed her alleged FaceTime relationship with Alex Rodriguez, who was engaged to Jennifer Lopez at the time. The pair called off their engagement in March 2021.

The reality star moved on with Brett Randle, announcing in October 2021 that they were engaged. Us confirmed in November 2022 that the twosome tied the knot in Mexico, days after an intimate courthouse ceremony.

Prior to finding fame, LeCroy was married to Josh Hughes. The exes share son Hudson.
