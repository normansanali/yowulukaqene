Inside The Life Of Popular Youtuber JonTron: Biography, Net Worth &amp; More

Inside The Life Of Popular Youtuber JonTron: Biography, Net Worth & More- The 33-year-old American YouTube star JonTron is widely known by his internet handle JonTron, his YouTube videos consist of comedically reviewed gameplay of poorly received games.

He also co-founded the website Normal Boots. In September of 2017, he posted a spoof advertisement for Flex Tape which has received over 66 million views.

Who is JonTron?

On March 24, 1990, JonTron whose birth name is Jonathan Jafari was born in Rancho Palos Verdes, California in the United States of America to his father, Afshin Jafari, and mother, Irene Jafari.

Concerning his career, he received his high school education at Palos Verdes Peninsula High School in 2004 and graduated in 2008. While studying at the Palos Verdes High School, he met some of his colleagues and YouTubers, such as Jirard Khalil and Barry Kramer.

What is JonTron’s age, height, and weight?

Presently, JonTron is 33 years old, born on March 24, 1990. His birth sign indicates that he is an Aries. JonTron stands at a height of 5′ 10′′ in feet inches and 178 cm in centimeters, he weighs 152 pounds and 69 kg.

What is JonTron’s net worth?

Being rated as one of the highest-paid YouTubers he’s been able to amass an estimated net worth of approximately $10-$15 million.

What is JonTron’s Nationality and Ethnicity?

He holds an American nationality and belongs to a mixed ethnicity of Hungarian and Iranian.

What is JonTron’s profession?

JonTron created a Newgrounds account under the moniker BirdmanXZ6 in the year 2003, shortly after creating the account, he published five anime clips depicting anthropomorphic onions. Later, in 2006, he created a YouTube account which he also named BirdmanXZ6.

He streamed and reviewed games on streaming platforms such as Screwattack.com. His first video on the platform was released in 2010. He reviewed games such as GoldenEye 007, it was also at this time he met with PeanutButterGamer, also known as Austin Hargrave, and they began to collaborate on their videos.In the same year, 2010, JonTron created a new YouTube channel, which he called the JonTronShow.

In May 2015, JonTron released a spin-off web series on his YouTube channel titled JonTron’s StarCade (often referred to as StarCade), in which he reviews games based on the Star Wars franchise.

He sometimes uploads videos with a voice-over addressing issues he deemed important and felt should be addressed. Although, most of the commentary is related to gaming.

Why was JonTron removed from Game Grumps?

JonTron couldn’t create enough content for his channel as well as Game Grumps resulting in him being removed.

Who is JonTron married to?

The YouTube star is happily married to his loving wife Charlotte Claw, also known as The Bear Claw. They tied the knot in a wedding ceremony on October 23rd, 2019.

Does JonTron have children?

There’s no information that indicates that JonTron has kids.
