The Tragic Story Of Alexis Eddy From 'Are You The One?'

Fans were introduced to Alexis Eddy in Season 6 of MTV's "Are You The One," which aired in 2017. Tragically, her life was cut short a few years after she appeared in the dating competition. On January 9, 2020, TMZ reported that police received an early morning call for a young woman in cardiac arrest. The 23-year-old was found dead at the West Virginia home.

Her grandmother, Carol Efaw, later revealed Eddy's cause of death to Radar. "It was an OD," she told the outlet. "She caught a hold of it again." Efaw blamed "Are You The One?" for exacerbating Eddy's struggles, telling the outlet "those shows mess up kids." She added, "My son and daughter-in-law are devastated."

Eddy had often shared about her recovery on social media, and in September 2019, she posted a photo of her and her dad. "Here's the first picture of my father and me, both sober and healthy, we've ever been blessed enough to take," she wrote. "Overwhelmed with gratefulness today." She added the hashtag #RecoveryRocks. Shortly before that, Eddy posted a photo outside of a Community Recovery Center and added the caption, "Recovery at its finest Gr8ful 2 b clean & sober #justfortoday." So it was obviously very important to her.
