Katie Holmes Wears Oversized Pants and Winter Sweater in the Summer Heat

The heat clearly isn't bothering Katie Holmes.

This past weekend, the Dawson's Creek alum was spotted in New York City wearing an outfit that seemed to be revving up for the forthcoming colder seasons. She looked cozy in a classic light gray knit sweater with a red-and-cream pattern across the chest and shoulders. She paired the unusual summer wardrobe piece with baggy black trousers and a brown leather cross-body bag.

Holmes's boyfriend, Bobby Wooten III, accompanied her during her walk. He looked stylish in light green trousers, a white T-shirt, a gray button-up, and black sunglasses.

Splash News

The couple was spotted embarking on another New York City date over the weekend. For the occasion, Holmes showed off her style in a black collared crop top with crisscrossing straps across the abdomen, paired with high-waisted and wide-leg trousers in a slate green hue. She also wore a maroon leather bucket bag from A.P.C. and black sneakers.

The actress seems to have an affinity for dressing against the weather. During last month's 90-degree heat wave on the East Coast, Holmes stepped out for a Good Morning America appearance wearing a textured knit midi dress from Chloé—which she paired with a huge matching knit shawl wrapped around her shoulders.

Holmes previously spoke to BAZAAR.com about her personal style.

"I love my T-shirts, and I love my vintage T-shirts. They're so yummy. They're a little dirty, a little stained or whatever, and that's me," she said.
