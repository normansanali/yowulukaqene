Daniel Bryan Debuts New 'Organic' WWE Championship Title

Daniel Bryan kept surprising the WWE Universe. This time, he debuted a brand new version of the WWE Championship made of organic hemp.

In a segment that first started with Bryan throwing the current WWE Championship into the garbage and calling it "trash", Bryan and his new "intellectual peer", Rowan, decided to debut a new title: one that was made of 100% pure hemp and wood from a naturally fallen oak tree.

The belt actually has quite an interesting look and it's quite eye-catching but the point was that Bryan believed the old belt represented everything that was wrong with the current state of WWE and this new belt — his belt — much better represented the message WWE should be sending.

WWE then later showed a much closer look at the belt on their Instagram feed.

"I said, when I became WWE Champion, I was going to change the world. But to change the world, we need new symbols!" Bryan exclaimed. "This is the new symbol of excellence. This is your new WWE Championship!"

As the segment rolled along, Bryan was interrupted by AJ Styles and then eventually Triple H who explained that Bryan would be defending that new championship in the Elimination Chamber against Randy Orton, Samoa Joe, AJ Styles, Jeff Hardy and Mustafa Ali.

What This Means

This is another unique way of making Bryan's character stand out as the better-than-everyone else type heel. It's a creative twist by WWE and it certainly has created a memorable moment that will go down in history on WWE reels and trivia shows from here until the end of time. Bryan is quickly creating a legacy and ensuring people forget that just months ago, he had entire arenas chanting Yes! for him as the most popular babyface in the company.
