Who Was Model Tommy Playboy? What Happened to Tommy Playboy?

Thomas Blackwell aka Tommy Playboy, a missing New York model, was found dead today. Let’s see what happened to the NYC model in detail. 

Thomas Blackwell Missing:

Thomas Blackwell aka Thomas Playboy from New York who went missing for more than 3 days was finally found dead today. He was described as having brown-colored eyes with 5’10 height. 

Having Chinese sign on the face and weighs 140 lbs. He was last seen in 59th St. (Colombus Circle) wearing the colorful boots with fur and fishnets, along with the furry outfit.

One of his followers, Bash Hartt, says,  “Tommy having you as a friend was a blessing I’m happy for all the good times we had.

How did Tommy Playboy die?

Thomas Blackwell, a model from New York passed away suddenly. One of his friend, indigo_agape wrote, “Oh no… this is so sad. Such a beautiful soul. He was unapologetically himself and carried himself with such confidence even though he questioned himself. I remember taking him under my wig when he first came to NY. I gave him a dancing gig working with me.”

“He was such a sweetheart. My lil brother I am going to miss you tremendously we were suppose to link up but God had other plans for you with all your modeling and dancing. I’m so happy I was able to tell you how proud I was of you. Gone too too soon. ❤️❤️my condolences to his family. This is heartbreaking”

However, Agape did not disclosed his cause and manner of death. His friends and colleagues shared their heartfelt tribute to him on social media.

Who was Thomas Blackwell?

Thomas started his career as a photographer in the New York City and later became a model. He was model for mefeater fashion show.

Tommy danced in Thalia’s music video, ‘La Luz’. He directed one of his friend’s music videos for Annalise Azadian’s “Life of the Party,” which he had a blast filming in New York.

Additionally, he worked in Chynna Roger’s short video, Drug Opera + film. He also worked with Telfar Clemens, Shayne Oliver, and Nicole Moudaber.

A friend of Thomas says that Tommy was one of the first individuals to believe in my potential as a photographer.

Tommy had not yet laid a solid foundation for their modeling profession in New York City at the time. Between us, there was an instant bond; Tommy had the gift of bringing out the best in others. 

Thomas Blackwell cause of death:

Thomas Blackwell who went missing was found dead. He has been depressed for 3 years, as per his friends’ post.

According to police, the man jumped from a northbound 2 train nearing the West 50th Street subway stop around 10:40 p.m.
