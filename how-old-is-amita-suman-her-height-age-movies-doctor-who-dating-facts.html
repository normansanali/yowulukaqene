Besides as the home of Mount Everest and natural serenity, Nepal has been a proud parent of an exceptional talent like Amita Suman. The cunning actress moved to England at an early age to pitch her career in the world of entertainment.

Amita Suman is a Nepali-born actress who is best known for her role as Naya in the fantasy-adventure series, The Outpost. She is set to make an appearance in the upcoming Netflix series, Shadow and Bone.

Amita Suman Bio & Family: Age, Birthday, Education, Height

The actress was born as Amita Suman in Nepal in the year 1997 which makes her 23 years of age. Amita celebrates her birthday on 19th July every year. Amita likes to keep her family’s whereabouts on a low-key note. However, she often shares snaps with her parents and grandparents on her Instagram account.

Amita was 7 when she moved to Brighton, England with her sister and her parents. For her education, Amita attended Sussex Downs College. Following that, she joined the Academy of Live and Recorded Arts (ALRA) and graduated in 2018.

About 5 feet and 7 inches (1.7 meters) tall in height, Amita’s birth sign is Cancer.

Amita’s Career In TV Shows, Net Worth

Soon after her graduation, Amita appeared in two episodes of the British medical drama show titled Casualty as Nina Biswas in 2018. The same year, she made a presence as Sameera in the single episode of the comedy-drama series, Ackley Bridge.

After impressing everyone with her initial performances, Amita registered a cameo role in the popular sci-fi series named Doctor Who in 2018. A year later, she had a recurring role as Naya in the second season of the American fantasy-adventure drama television series, The Outpost.

Amita looks set to appear as Inej Ghafa in the 2021 release-set Netflix fantasy television series, Shadow and Bone. From her professional career, Amita certainly bags a compelling net worth which remains under review as of now.
