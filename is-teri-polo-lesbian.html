Is Teri Polo lesbian?

Theresa Elizabeth Polo is an American actress. Polo starred as Pamela Martha Focker in the Meet the Parents trilogy and played the role of police officer Stef Adams Foster in the Freeform series The Fosters and its spinoff Good Trouble.

Is Teri Polo lesbian?

Theresa is not lesbian, since she has dated several men. Polo married photographer Anthony Moore. They have a son together, Griffin (born 2002). In 2003, Polo and Moore divorced. In 2006, while on the set of a video, Polo met drummer Jamie Wollam. They moved in together and had a daughter, Bayley, in 2007, but broke up in 2012.

Polo’s acting debut, in 1987, was in the role of Kristin Larsen on the ABC daytime soap opera Loving. The following year, she earned a role in primetime on the short-lived CBS series TV 101. She also starred in the 1990 miniseries The Phantom of the Opera as Christine Daaé.

Polo posed nude for Playboy magazine. She was ranked No. 40 on the Maxim Hot 100 Women of 2002. Polo was also featured in InStyle. Polo also starred in the Lifetime Movie Network 2006 movie, Legacy of Fear. In 2007, she starred in a Hallmark Channel movie Love is a Four Letter Word.
