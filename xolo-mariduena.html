All About the Blue Beetle Actor Xolo Mariduena

Xolo Mariduena (age: 22, born on June 9, 2001) is an American actor who is known for his work in productions like “Wu-Tang: An American Saga” and “Fast & Furious Spy Racers.”

Most people know Xolo because of his performance as Miguel Diaz in the popular Netflix series “Cobra Kai.” He has also worked in the series “Rush Hour” (2016) alongside actors like Justin Hires and Jonathan Patrick Foo.

For those who do not know, “Cobra Kai” is an action comedy involving middle-aged martial arts rivals Johnny Lawrence and Daniel LaRusso.

The show is filled with young stars like Tanner Buchanan, Mary Mouser, Courtney Henggeler, Jacob Bertrand, and Gianni DeCenzo. In 2022, the fifth season of “Cobra Kai” came out on Netflix on September 9 and quickly became everyone’s favorite.

On the one hand, where Maridueña is earning fame for his performance in the famous “Cobra Kai,” On the other hand, Xolo has been gaining attention for making his surprise landing in the new DC movie “Blue Beetle” in which he played the role of Jaime Reyes.

Xolo Mariduena is Mexican Descent

On June 9, 2001, Xolo Mariduena (age 22) took birth in Los Angeles, California, U.S. Sources revealed that Xolo’s father, Omar G. Ramírez, is a Cuban music producer, whereas his mother, Carmelita Ramírez Sánchez is a Mexican radio jockey.

While studying at Cathedral High School, he used to participate in stage shows because, unlike his father, he has always been more interested in acting than music.

His parents knew that their child was talented, which is why they used to take him to audition for shows and movies.

In 2012 the 11-year-old Xo cleared the audition for “Parenthood.” He played a recurring character named Victor Graham in this Lawrence Trilling’s comedy-drama. The following year he landed a significant part in one more show called “Major Crimes.”

Xolo Mariduena Journey to Become a Star

The young Mariduena worked in numerous shows from 2012 to 2017, including “Furst Born,” “Mack & Moxy,” “Rush Hour,” and “Twin Peaks.”

In 2019 he completed his school and decided to join the prestigious Young Actors Space. At YAS, Xolo received the mentorship of talented acting coaches who taught him various acting techniques.

Besides acting, Maridueña is also a skilled voice artist. He gave his voice to multiple characters in animated shows like Lucas in “Fast & Furious Spy Racers” and Zaid in “Cleopatra in Space.” He also worked from 2019 to 2021 for a children’s show titled “Victor & Valentino.”

The actual acting journey of Xolo started with “Cobra Kai.” It helped him earn recognition as an adult actor because people used to recognize him as a child artist before this show.


Xolo Mariduena’s Girlfriend and Love Life

In 2018 while shooting for Cobra Kai’s first season Xolo and Hannah Kepple became good friends. Slowly the friendship between these young stars turned into a love affair.

Kepple and Mariduena dated each other for a couple of years. In 2021 during an interview, Xo revealed that he is single and not dating anybody; since then, he has not been in any romantic relationship.
