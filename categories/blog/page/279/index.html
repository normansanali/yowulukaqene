Drakes Admission About His Encounter with Swedish Police

published on August 19, 2024
After initially denying the incident, Drake has now confirmed that he had a run-in with Swedish police. The Canadian rapper shared a series of photos on Sunday, July 17, chronicling his recent experiences. Among the images he posted was a picture of a document given to him by Swedish authorities, also known as Polisen. The document is titled “Information for Those Suspected of a Crime and Subsequently Detained.” It outlines Drake’s rights under Swedish law, such as the right to be informed of the reasons for his detention and the right to legal representation.
