Leah McSweeney, the American reality actress, businesswoman, and fashion designer, is best known as the founder of the women's clothing line called Married to the Mob.

The adorable media personality Leah has appeared in the episode of the Bravo's reality show Millionaire's Matchmaker and as the main cast of The Real Housewives of New York City (RHONY).

Early Life 

Leah Christine McSweeney was born on August 27, 1982, in New York, the United States of America. She was raised by Bryan McSweeney (father) and his wife Bunny (mother) alongside her sister Sarah McSweeney, and a brother named Daniel McSweeney. Leah & Sarah's parents are married since 1971.

Similarly, Leah's sister Sarah is a model, investigator, and a singer by profession. She has released the songs like Pura Vida, Ignite the Fire, etc. Sarah is married to Russell Steinberg, with whom she has shared a daughter named Cecelia Marie.

Concerning Leah's education, she attended Convent of the Sacred Heart, a Roman Catholic all-girls school located in Manhattan. Similarly, McSweeney studied at the Fashion Institute of Technology, a part of the State University of Ne York.
