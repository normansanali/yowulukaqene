Grace Randolph Net Worth

In 2009, the first comic book Randolph wrote was DC Comics's Justice League Unlimited. She went on to write Boom! Studios' Muppet Peter Pan, which was a reinterpretation of Peter Pan as a Muppet story. In 2010, she wrote Her-oes, a comic book mini-series about the teenage years of some of Marvel Comics' female Avengers. In 2011, she wrote for Archaia's Fraggle Rock comic.
