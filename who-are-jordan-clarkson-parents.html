Who Is Jordan Clarkson ?His Bio,Parents,Net worth And Dating History.

Jordan Clarkson is a talented Filipino-American basketball player.

He plays as a guard for the Utah Jazz and previously played for the Cleveland Cavaliers and the Los Angeles Lakers.

He was named NBA Sixth Man of the Year in 2021 and made the NBA All-Rookie First Team in 2015.

His father encouraged him to become a well-rounded player.

Jordan Clarkson Parents Ethnicity

Jordan Clarkson’s father, Mike, is African-American, while his mother, Annette Tullao Davis, is Filipina-American.

His maternal grandmother, Marcelina Tullao Kingsolver, hails from Bacolor, Pampanga, Philippines.

Born in Florida, Jordan’s parents met while serving in the United States Air Force.

After his parents’ separation when he was young, he was primarily raised by his mother.

At the age of six, he moved to San Antonio, Texas.

Jordan Clarkson identifies as American-Filipino and holds the distinction of being the first Asian-American starting backcourt player in NBA history.

He has represented the Philippines in international events such as the 2016 FIBA Olympic Qualifying Tournament and the 2015 FIBA Asia Championship.

Jordan proudly embraces his Filipino heritage and feels honored to represent the Philippines in basketball.
