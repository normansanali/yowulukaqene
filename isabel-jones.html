Experience

Isabel is a Brooklyn-based writer and editor with a special interest in pop culture. She worked at InStyle for nearly six years, most recently serving as InStyle's entertainment and celebrity news writer. Prior to working at InStyle, Isabel attended New York University and held internships at various magazines, including Cosmopolitan, NYLON, and Us Weekly. She currently works as a manager for editorial services and corporate communications at CBS.

Education

Isabel graduated from New York University’s Gallatin School in 2016, where she studied non-fiction writing and pop culture analysis.
