Eminem U-Turns On Ben Johnson Diss Track After Lions Coach Stays

Eminem has had a change of heart on releasing a “diss track” aimed at Ben Johnson after the Detroit Lions offensive coordinator announced he was staying with the team.

Johnson was a head coach candidate for a number of other NFL teams including the Seattle Seahawks and Washington Commanders, but informed the league that he would be staying with the Lions after they reached the NFC Conference game, falling just short of the Super Bowl.

Eminem, a noted Detroit Lions fan, welcomed the news on social media and jokingly revealed he would no longer be dropping a diss track against the coach.

“Guess I should stop recording this diss track..!! Lol!!” he wrote on X. “@ Ben Johnson Thank you for staying!!! We can’t break this team up ESPECIALLY since I’M on it!!! Coach Campbell we love you bro!!!”

He added: “Let’s run it back next year!!…I’m READY!!!! I promise you I WILL get us to the super bowl!!!”

The rap legend even offered to suit up for the team, saying in a video: “Yo, Dan! I’m reporting! I’m gonna report, third quarter. I’m reporting, offensive line. Eligible receiver. I’ll be the quarterback. The entire line, offensive line […] and I will throw it to myself, and I will score a touchdown. In the third quarter.”
