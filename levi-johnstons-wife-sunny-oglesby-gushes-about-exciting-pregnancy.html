Levi Johnstons Wife Sunny Oglesby Gushes About Exciting Pregnancy

A baby bump makes for the perfect pillow. Levi Johnston’s pregnant wife, Sunny Oglesby, posted a photo of their 3-year-old daughter, Indy Rae, snuggling up to her belly on Thursday, November 29. 

“Thankful for all the tummy hugs I get every day now,” the teacher-turned-real estate agent gushed on Instagram Story. “They will randomly lift up my shirt to kiss my stomach in public lol. The kids have made it such an exciting pregnancy because they are now old enough to be understand and be excited about another sibling.”

Oglesby, 26, and Johnston, 28, are also  parents of daughter  Breeze, 6. The Alaska-based electrician shares son, Tripp, 9, with his ex-fiancée Bristol Palin. 

The couple, who have been married for six years, announced on November 24 that they are expecting “baby Johnston #4” in July. For the Instagram photo, the whole family wore matching red flannel pajamas. 

Johnston and Oglesby tied the knot in October 2012 in Wasilla, Alaska, after just over a year of dating. The Playgirl model —who named his daughter Breeze Beretta for the Italian firearm manufacturer —wore a tuxedo with a camouflage vest and bow tie. 

While Johnston’s relationship with Palin, 28, didn’t work out — they announced their engagement in 2010, but split weeks later — the two remain close.

“I think Levi’s incredible. I have nothing but great things to say about Levi as a father,” the Teen Mom OG star told Us Weekly last month. “I consider him a friend. I have nothing but great things to say about Sunny. She’s a loving mother. She cares. I will continue my friendship with them.”

Palin went on to have kids Sailor, 2, and Atlee, 18 months, with military veteran Dakota Meyer. In August, they ended their on-off marriage of less than two years.
