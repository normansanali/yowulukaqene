Alya Manasa Wiki/Biography

The actress was brought into existence in 1992 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. She completed her early schooling from Campion Anglo-Indian Higher Secondary School, Tiruchirapalli, Tamil Nadu. After that she enrolled herself at Ethiraj College for Women, Chennai where she completed her graduation course.

She was born and brought up in a middle-class Hindu family in Tamil Nadu, Since her childhood days she was inclined towards dancing and acting and has a strong desire of becoming a successful actress.
