The Meaning Behind The Song: Sic Transit Gloria Glory Fades by Brand New

Introduction

Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades by Brand New is one of the most iconic tracks of the early 2000s emo scene. With its haunting lyrics and powerful instrumentation, it struck a chord with many young people grappling with the complexities of heartbreak and loss. But what exactly is the meaning behind the song? In this article, we’ll dive deep into the anatomy of heartbreak and explore the true significance of this emotional masterpiece.

The Story of Sic Transit Gloria

At its core, Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades is a song about unrequited love. Frontman Jesse Lacey wrote the track after a failed attempt at pursuing a girl he was deeply infatuated with. He found out that she was actually seeing someone else and it devastated him.

The title of the song is actually a quote from the Latin phrase “Sic Transit Gloria Mundi,” which translates to “Thus Passes the Glory of the World.” The phrase is often used to remind people of the fleeting nature of fame and success, and how ultimately all things must come to an end. In the context of the song, it could be interpreted as a warning to not put too much stock in a romantic relationship, as even the most intense love affairs can easily crumble and fade away.

The Lyrics: Analyzing the Heartbreak

Let’s take a closer look at some of the lyrics of the song and what they really mean.

“She said, ‘Use your hands in my spare time. We’ve got one thing in common, it’s this tongue of mine.'”

This verse is clearly sexual in nature, and it shows how the narrator is willing to do anything to gain the affection of the girl he desires. He knows that they share a physical attraction, but he’s willing to overlook any incompatibilities they may have (such as differing personalities or values) just to be with her.

“Can I still get into heaven if I kill myself? Can I still get into heaven if I kill myself?”

These lines are particularly poignant, as they show the narrator’s despair and hopelessness in the face of heartbreak. He’s asking if there’s any way he can escape the pain of unrequited love, even if it means taking his own life. It’s a raw and vulnerable moment that many listeners can relate to.

“At the bottom of the ocean fish won’t judge you by your faults.”

This lyric is a bit more cryptic, but it suggests that the narrator feels like he’s drowning in his pain and that nobody understands him. He longs for a place where he can escape judgement and just be himself.

The Music: Setting the Mood for Heartbreak

The music of Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades is just as important as the lyrics in conveying the emotions of heartbreak. The track begins with a simple bassline that gradually builds in intensity as the drums and guitars kick in. The instrumentation is both melancholic and aggressive, perfectly capturing the conflicting feelings of sadness and anger that often accompany heartbreak.

The bridge of the song is particularly powerful, as the tempo slows down and Lacey delivers the heart-wrenching lines:

“‘The truth is, you could slit my throat, and with my one last gasping breath, I’d apologize for bleeding on your shirt.'”

This verse perfectly encapsulates the complicated feelings of pain, love, and guilt that often consume us after a break-up. The music builds even further until it reaches its explosive and cathartic finale, leaving listeners feeling both drained and uplifted.

The Legacy of Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades

Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades has become an anthem of sorts for people grappling with heartbreak. Its raw and honest lyrics have resonated with countless fans over the years, and it continues to be a staple of emo playlists and karaoke bars.

But the song’s legacy extends beyond just its popularity. It has also inspired countless other artists to explore the complexities of heartbreak in their own music. Without Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades, we may not have some of the other iconic break-up songs that have graced the airwaves over the years.

Conclusion

Heartbreak is a universal experience that we all go through at some point in our lives. Sic Transit Gloria… Glory Fades is a powerful reminder of just how painful and complex those feelings can be. With its haunting lyrics and aggressive instrumentation, this track has captured the hearts of millions and will continue to be a touchstone for generations of emo fans to come.
