Was Chuck Norris in Any Bruce Lee Movies?

Bruce Lee and Chuck Norris were two of the biggest legends in martial arts firearms at the time so their on-screen appearance together has always been under scrutiny by many fans and enthusiasts of this genre Cinema.

The Way of the Dragon: Chuck Norris

Yes, Chuck Norris, a Bruce Lee film and an American actor and marital artist. He appeared in the 1972 action comedy The Way of the Dragon, which starred Bruce Lee.

Norris portrayed Colt, a proficient fighter brought in by a criminal organization to neutralize the threat represented by Bruce Lees Tang Lung in the film.

The movie was Norris's film debut and went on to become the highest-grossing movie in Hong Kong history, at that time.

The Iconic Fight Scene

The standout moment of 'The Way of the Dragon' was undoubtedly the legendary fight scene involving Bruce Lee and Chuck Norris.

This fight scene is also often considered one of the all-time best and most iconic live-action fight scenes in film history.

Of course, it was shot without a permit Bruce Lee allegedly bribed certain officials to give him maybe an hour to shoot. The fact that the filming took place almost entirely illegally adds another layer to the historical importance of this movie!

Chuck Norris' Take on the Collaboration

Archival footage of the legendary Chuck Norris talking about the success of "The Way of the Dragon" where he reveals that the film was made for $145,000 and ended up making over $60 million worldwide back in the day, around $700+ million now days when adjusted.

Norris spoke of his love for working with Bruce Lee in the movie and that the fight scene was awesome fun to make.

He also refers to the fight scene as a martial arts classic, similar to his response in the video for their collaboration.
